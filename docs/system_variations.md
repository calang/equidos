# Variations

This document considers possible variations on training and operational conditions for the equid identification system.

## Data Variations

1. Angle of view of the animal's face
   - front
   - right side
   - left side
1. Face area to use as input
   - full face
   - muzzle only
1. Accesories on the animal
   - no accessories
   - mozzle or bridle
2. Cropping
   - face covering a large, maximal area of the image, with minimal area around the face
   - face covering a less-than-maximal area of the image, with large area (>20%) around the face
4. Lighting Conditions
   - well-lit images
   - low-light images
   - images with shadows or uneven lighting
5. Image Quality
   - high-resolution images only, no motion blur
   - high- and low-resolution images 
   - high- and low-resolution images, some with motion blur
3. Species Diversity
   - horses only
   - horses, donkeys and mules
